Food Delivery Profitability & Operations Analysis
◦ Analyzed 1000 food delivery orders using Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.
◦ Performed profitability modeling identifying Rs.105K operational loss.
◦ Discovered discounts contributed 45% of total operational cost.
◦ Identified peak demand hours (7PM–10PM) through time-series analysis.
◦ Simulated commission and discount optimization improving profit margins
